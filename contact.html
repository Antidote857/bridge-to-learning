Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some quick answers to common questions.

What services does Bridge-to-Learning offer?

We provide school support, inclusive education guidance, leadership support, curriculum assistance, and inspection readiness consultation.

How quickly do you respond to enquiries?

We aim to respond to all messages within 24 hours during business days.

Can schools request customised support?

Yes. We tailor our support based on the needs of each school, leadership team, or educational programme.

Do you offer consultation outside Bulawayo?

Yes. Depending on the need, support can be arranged for schools and institutions in other locations as well.
